MY MISSION

I have been an Aveda-trained colorist in the Ann Arbor community for 10 years.
I believe in delivering a quality experience with professional service.
The relationships I have built over this time are very important to me and I would
love the opportunity to build a relationship with you and take care
of your haircolor needs.

My area of expertise is color with a special interest in highlights. I believe healthy
hair is the foundation of beautiful color and as your colorist, the health and integrity
of your hair will always be my primary concern.
